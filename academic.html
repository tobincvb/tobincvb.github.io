Research & Papers

Twisted Topological Hochschild Homology (2025)

My Honours thesis exploring equivariant stable homotopy theory and twisted topological cyclic homology. Develops a twisted model of p-cyclotomic spectra and provides an identification between norms and twisted cyclic bar constructions.

Odd Primary Hopf Invariant One

Vector Bundles and K Theory Report: Exposition and seminar talk on the Hopf Invariant one problem, extending the maps from Steenrod squares to Steenrod powers to give an odd Hopf Invariant.

Read Report →
Applications of Differential Forms to Homotopy Theory

Differential Forms in Algebraic Topology Report: An extensive report detailing relevant background including homotopy theory and differential forms basics. Explores the relationship between the two topics through differential graded algebras and minimal models.

Lévy Integration

Stochastic Analysis Report: Report and seminar talk discussing Poisson integration, Lévy-Itô decomposition and Lévy stochastic integrals, detailing a worked example and financial applications.
