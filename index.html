About Me

I hold a double degree in mathematics and statistics from the Australian National University, graduating in 2025 with First Class Honours. I commenced university at age 15 and completed my Honours year at 20.

My thesis, titled 'Twisted Topological Hochschild Homology', explored equivariant stable homotopy theory and twisted topological cyclic homology under the supervision of Dr. Vigleik Angeltveit.

Currently, I am working as a Data Analyst at Evolve Capabilities, applying my quantitative and analytical skills to solve complex problems in an industry setting.
