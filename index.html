About Me

Hi, I'm Tobin Carlton Van Buizen. I have an honours degree in mathematics and a degree in statistics, both from The Australian National University.

I completed my honours degree in 2025 with First Class Honours under the supervision of Dr. Vigleik Angeltveit on equivariant stable homotopy theory, twisted topological Hochschild homology, and twisted topological cyclic homology. You can read my thesis, titled 'Twisted Topological Hochschild Homology', here.
